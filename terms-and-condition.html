Rules for Guests

  • Check out time is 11 AM. Check-in time at 12 Noon.

  • Breakfast is served between 8:00 AM and 10:00 AM in the morning.

  • Lunch is served between 12:30 PM and 2:30 PM in the afternoon.

  • Dinner is served between 8 PM and 10 PM in the evening.

  • Food served will be as per the menu. Additional food if any must be ordered at least 2 hours in advance.

  • Guests must lock their doors and windows when not in the room. Management will not be liable for loss or damage to the guests' belongings.

  • Do not wash the cloth in the Toilet.

  • Please maintain silence. Do not shout, play music, blow a horn or make noise.

  • Keep fire away. Do not kindle fire, Do not throw away lighted matches, cigarettes etc.

  • Keep the area clean. Do not litter plastic, paper, etc.

  • Do not hunt, destroy, remove or collect any forest produce.

Booking and Cancellation Rules

  • Booking will be confirmed only after payment of the full amount in advance.

  • Cancellation and refund rules.

  • Charges are subject to a minimum cancellation amount of Rs. 1000.

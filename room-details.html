Aishwarya Homestay
Studio Apartment

Our homestay is on the first floor of our occupant residence with an independent staircase.

Merely 100 meters from the National Highway NH544 at Chithali Palam provides a quick family resting place while travelling this stretch between Alathur and Coimbatore, with ample private parking space.

The living room, bedroom and kitchen have big windows to help you take a broad view of the village around and paddy fields beyond. We offer a big bed with clean linen and have an attached independent spacious bathroom with modern amenities.

Fast WIFI connection with satellite TV and international socket for any appliances that you carry.

The balcony in the front provides the view of the village life overlooking the village road and the backside balcony provides view of the nearby vegetable garden.

The building is equipped with solar power taking into consideration of the nature's bounty and battery backup. CCTV cameras at the front and rear provides security for the premises.

The kitchen is equipped with toaster, mini refregerator and a kettle. An electric induction heater is available on demand.

We are pet friendly and have our kennel available at an extra cost which was used by our own Rio the labrador.

We have one double bed. Additional extra two single cots and beds are available at extra cost on demand.

Family-Friendly Accommodations

We welcome children, with a maximum of two per booking, and kids stay for free! Children can stay at no extra cost when using existing bedding. Additionally, we offer individual single cots (up to 2) and bedding upon request.

Culinary Delights

We offer on-demand services for home-cooked South Indian food with prior intimation.

For typical Kerala non-vegetarian dishes, you can visit Malabar Hotel Erimayur. For South Indian vegetarian delicacies, Sri Mu Saravana Bhavan - Erimayur is a nearby home-deliverable destination.

Activities to Enjoy

  • Explore the Nature Trail: Enjoy a leisurely walk or bike ride through our scenic nature trail, immersing yourself in the beauty of the natural surroundings.

  • Swim in the Private Natural Pond: Take a refreshing dip in our serene, private pond, nestled in a tranquil natural setting.

  • Cycle Through Scenic Trails: Embark on a 15-kilometer cycling adventure along picturesque trails that wind through lush paddy fields and the winding Malampuzha irrigation canal.

  • Stroll Through Hillock and Peacock Sanctuary: Experience a delightful nature walk with breathtaking views of the hillock and the nearby peacock sanctuary.

  • Experience the Magic of Nature: In our backyard, you're in for a delightful treat! With a bit of luck, you might catch a glimpse of vibrant butterflies, graceful turtles, majestic peacocks, enchanting cuckoos, and striking kingfishers. Each visit offers a chance to witness these beautiful creatures in their natural habitat.
